The composition of `ModalDialog` for marketing style scenarios. This component passes through all of its props to an underlying `ModalDialog` component providing some limited customization. If you have unique needs, use the `ModalDialog` compound component family directly.

```jsx live

        isOpen={isOpen}
        onClose={close}
        heroIsDark
        heroNode={(
          <ModalDialog.Hero>
            <ModalDialog.Hero.Background
              backgroundSrc="https://source.unsplash.com/1600x800/?nature,flower"

        footerNode={(
```
