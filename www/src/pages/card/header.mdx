### Card.Header

##### Basic Usage

```jsx live
<Card>
  <Card.Header>
    <Card.Header.Title>Card Title</Card.Header.Title>
  </Card.Header>
</Card>
```

##### Small Variant
```jsx live
<Card>
  <Card.Header>
    <Card.Header.Title small>
      Card Title
    </Card.Header.Title>
  </Card.Header>
</Card>
```

##### Custom Title Sizes
```jsx live
<Card>
  <Card.Header>
    <Card.Header.Title sizes={{ default: 'h2', small: 'h3' }}>
      Card Title
    </Card.Header.Title>
  </Card.Header>
</Card>
```
